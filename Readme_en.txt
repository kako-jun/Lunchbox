================================================================================
# Lunchbox
================================================================================
Thank you for your interest.

'Lunchbox' is a tool that launches folders and apps.
It works on startup of windows or at the completion of Dropbox synchronization
automatically.
Folders opened by this tool are shown tiled.

By using this tool, you don't worry about the collision of files that managed
by Dropbox.

By simple configuration, you can also use this tool to detect the end of the
YouTube video playback, and automatically shutdown your PC.

Sincerely,
The Jpico Team
